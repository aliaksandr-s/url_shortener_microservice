URL Shortener Microservice

https://url-shrtnr-microservice.herokuapp.com/new/http://google.com
https://url-shrtnr-microservice.herokuapp.com/new/http://foo.com:80

What you will get:

{ "full_url":"http://google.com","short_url":"http://url-shrtnr-microservice.herokuapp.com/V2j" }

How to use:

http://url-shrtnr-microservice.herokuapp.com/V2j

https://www.google.com/
